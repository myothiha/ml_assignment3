Input Features:

  • Brand: The company name that have produced your car.
  • Max Power: the highest amount of power that its engine can produce under optimal conditions.
  • Mileage: The fuel efficiency of your car which refers to the distance your car can travel on a specific amount of fuel.
  • Year: It is refered to the year in which the car was manufactured or produced.

How to use:

  • You don't provide any information regarding your car. Default values will be used.
  • Fill the information related to your car: Brand, Max Power, Mileage and Year.
  • You can leave blank if you don't information for a field. In that case, default values will be used.
